About me

I am a 5-th year Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Engineering from University of Minnesota (UMN), advised by Prof. Changhyun Choi. Before joining UMN, I received my Master's Degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering (Machine Learning and Data Science Track) from University of California San Diego (UCSD), USA, and a Bachelor's Degree from North China Electric Power University, China.

I will be graduating in 2025 and I am open for machine learning and robotics related job opportunities! Please drop me messages if you find me a fit candidate!
